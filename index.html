// 01 — About

Bridging the gap between theory and the workbench.

I'm a Senior Robotics Trainer, STEM Educator, and Soft Skills Trainer with a background in robotics education, electronics, and IoT fundamentals. My work centers on hands-on, project-based learning — helping students move from understanding a circuit diagram to actually building, debugging, and presenting the thing they made.
